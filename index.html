ComSSA is the Computer Science Students Association at Curtin University, operating out of Curtin's Bentley campus, in Perth, Western Australia. ComSSA is located on the ground floor of the Engineering Pavilion, building 215. Our office is also located in this building, in room 215.104.

We provide a wide range of services for our members, such as discounts for our regular social events, access to academic materials and assistance for Department of Computing units, and free Microsoft software! You can read more about our services here

Membership is open to everyone, you don't even have to be a student! We're also a Curtin Student Guild-affiliated club, so membership is free to Curtin Student Guild club voucher holders.

If you're interested in joining, check out our signup page! For any other enquiries, or just to have a chat, contact us:
