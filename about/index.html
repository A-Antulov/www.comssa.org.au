--- layout: default title: About ComSSA ---

Since 2002, ComSSA has been the home and second family to many computing enthusiasts and students at Curtin University.

Our aim is to foster a thriving community of future and active computing professionals while providing a place for like-minded members to study, connect and enjoy university life, as well as representation to the Department of Computing and the university as a whole.

You can read our constitution, which outlines the rules that ComSSA operates under.
